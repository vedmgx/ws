In-Depth Software Features

xGM is a modular platform offering high-performance 2D and 3D solutions for gravity and magnetic data analysis, inversion, and interpretation.

xGM-2D: Advanced Modeling & Analysis

Core 2D Capabilities:

  • 2D Forward & Inversion: Direct and inverse gravity/magnetics modeling for both block and gridded subsurface models.
  • High-Performance Computing: Multi-threaded processing utilizing CPU and GPU for accelerated performance.
  • Interactive Model Building: Real-time editing and adjustment of 2D block and gridded models.
  • Data Filtering & Transformation: Interactive 1D data filtering and calculation of potential field transformants (continuation, gradients).
  • Depth Estimation Tools: Deconvolution methods including Euler and Werner deconvolution.
  • Advanced Signal Processing: Comprehensive Wavelet analysis for signal decomposition and feature extraction.
  • Data Interoperability: Seamless import and export of industry-standard geophysical formats.

xGM-3D: Large-Scale Inversion & AI Interpretation

Advanced 3D & AI Features:

  • 3D Forward & Inversion: Direct and inverse gravity/magnetics modeling for complex 3D block and gridded models.
  • High-Speed Processing: Multi-threaded computation on CPU and GPU for massive 3D data volumes.
  • Advanced Data Processing: Interactive 2D filtering and calculation of potential field transformants (continuation, gradients).
  • 3D Model Refinement: Interactive editing of 3D block and gridded models.
  • AI Prospect Prediction: AI-powered forecasting of prospective areas and prediction of structural surfaces and missing data.
  • AI Zoning & Analysis: AI-driven regionalization (zoning) and deep statistical analysis in sliding windows.
  • Depth Estimation: Spectral analysis for estimating source depths based on the logarithm of the spectrum.
  • Industry Compatibility: Import and export of all standard geophysical data formats.

System Requirements

Minimum Requirements

  • OS: Windows 10/11 / Linux / MacOS
  • CPU: Intel Core i5-7200U or Equivalent
  • RAM: 4 GB (More may be required for large 2D/3D models)

Recommended Setup

  • OS: Windows 10/11 / Linux / MacOS
  • CPU: Intel Core i7 (12th Generation or Newer) or Equivalent
  • RAM: 16 GB (More may be required for large 2D/3D models)
  • GPU: Nvidia RTX 2060 or Newer (CUDA-enabled graphics cards only)
