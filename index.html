Why xGM?

xGM is designed for geophysicists, survey teams and researchers who need reliable, fast and scalable solutions for potential field inversion. Built from the ground up to exploit modern GPUs, xGM performs large-scale 3D gravity and magnetic inversions on irregular topography with industry-grade regularization and data-integration tools.

Use xGM to accelerate exploration workflows, obtain reproducible subsurface models, and reduce interpretation time with automated anomaly localization and AI-based pattern recognition.
